Recipies - World's Best scrambled Eggs

I adopted this recipie from a book called Sydney Food by Bill Granger.Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1st visit to Bill's restaurant in Kings Cross,
Sydney. I have been after the recipie. I have since transformed it into what i really believe are the best scrambled eggs i have ever tasted

This recipie is what I call a "very special breakfast"; just look at the ingredients to see why. It has to be tasted to be believed.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/3 cup of cream(2 3/4 ounces)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly miled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, cream, and salt in a bowl
  2. Melt the butter in a non stick pan over a high heat(taking care not to burn the butter).
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait untill it starts settling arounf the edge of the pan(around 20 seconds).
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet, and let it cook for another 20 seconds.
  5. Foid contents in again, leave it for 20 seconds, and repeat until the eggs are only just done.
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly miled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives.

Yous should make a maximum of two servings per frying pan.
